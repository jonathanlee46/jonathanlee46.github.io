Jonathan Lee

    I grew up in the California Bay Area, having always had a passion for technology, language, and sports. All throughout my childhood I have worked with, in, and around computers - I built my first PC when I had just entered middle school. Since then I have gone on to build many more, build a robust home network, and even build whole computer systems for dental offices and work as freelance IT.
    I studied Japanese Language and Culture at the University of California, Davis and graduated with a Bachelor of Arts. After that, I spent 3 years in the Nagano Prefecture of Japan furthering my learnings, and also teaching English.
    I also love sports, and I grew up a Bay Area native cheering for my teams. I especially love Football - feel free to come and chat to me about anything to do with it! I also love motorsports, especially motorcycle racing such as MotoGP and World Superbikes.
